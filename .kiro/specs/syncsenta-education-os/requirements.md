# Requirements Document

## Introduction

SyncSenta is a **Web4 Education Operating System** — a fully decentralized, AI-driven, immersive lifelong learning utility built on blockchain, IPFS, and self-sovereign identity from day 1. It begins in Kenya and scales globally by 2030. It replaces the rigid, standardized school model with a dynamic, hyper-personalized learning network grounded in the principle that **education is a common good, not a commodity**.

Built on a React/Next.js frontend, Rust Axum backend, Polygon blockchain layer, IPFS decentralized storage, W3C DID authentication, and progressive decentralization architecture, SyncSenta targets 100,000+ concurrent users in Kenya by 2026, 1M+ across East Africa by 2028, and 10M+ globally by 2030.

**Core Philosophy:** Learning as a Utility (LaaS) — as essential as electricity, as personal as a heartbeat. Cooperation over competition. Mastery over time. Lifelong and life-wide. Human-centered AI that nurtures imagination and creativity, not just repetitive skills. **User sovereignty:** learners own their identity, credentials, and data.

The platform introduces a seven-tier user hierarchy with approval workflows, Mwalimu AI 3.0 (neural-symbolic digital twin tutor), real-time virtual classrooms, blockchain micro-credentials from day 1, a Learn-to-Earn token economy, Social-Emotional Learning (SEL) integration, 31 background AI agents for holistic wellbeing, immersive VR/AR learning (2027+), and a path to full DAO governance by 2029.

**Success Metrics (2026):** Built on a React + TypeScript + Tailwind CSS + Shadcn/UI frontend with a Rust Axum backend, Polygon blockchain layer, IPFS storage, and W3C DID authentication, SyncSenta targets 100,000+ concurrent users across Kenya's Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) covering PP1 through Grade 12 (Pre-Primary 1 & 2, Grades 1-6, Junior Secondary School 1-3, Senior Secondary School 1-3).

The platform introduces a seven-tier user hierarchy with approval workflows, Mwalimu AI 2.0 (voice-enabled adaptive tutor powered by GPT-5.4, Gemini 3.1 Pro, ElevenLabs eleven_v3 TTS, and scribe_v2 STT), real-time virtual classrooms via Jitsi Meet with DID authentication, blockchain micro-credentials, SyncToken learn-to-earn economy, a parent engagement portal, M-Pesa fee payments via Daraja API, predictive analytics for at-risk student identification, a teacher-created content marketplace on IPFS, a comprehensive communication hub, and a Rust inference layer for offline-capable edge ML using the `candle` crate.

**Success Metrics:**
- 10,000+ active students in first 3 months
- 95%+ user satisfaction score
- <5% churn rate
- 80%+ lesson completion rate
- AI tutor accuracy >90%
- 100% of credentials on blockchain
- 100% of content on IPFS
- <2s DID resolution time

## Glossary

- **SyncSenta_System**: The complete SyncSenta 2.0 AI-powered education operating system platform
- **User**: Any authenticated person interacting with the system
- **Student**: A learner registered on the platform for educational content and activities
- **Parent**: A parent or guardian linked to one or more students to monitor progress
- **Teacher**: An educator who creates content, manages classes, and tracks student progress
- **School_Admin**: A school-level administrator managing enrollment, staff, and fees
- **School_Head**: A principal or head teacher who approves teachers and school admins within their school
- **County_Officer**: A county education officer who oversees schools within a county and approves school heads
- **National_Admin**: A super-administrator with system-wide access and oversight
- **Approval_Workflow**: The process by which a higher-tier user approves or rejects a lower-tier user's registration
- **Mwalimu_AI**: The AI-powered adaptive tutor component (version 2.0) providing voice, text, and multimodal learning support
- **Virtual_Classroom**: A real-time video conferencing session powered by Jitsi Meet for live instruction
- **Assessment**: A test, quiz, or evaluation activity to measure student understanding
- **Scheme**: A structured lesson plan aligned with CBC curriculum standards
- **Learning_Path**: A personalized sequence of content and activities generated by Mwalimu_AI based on student performance
- **Content_Library**: The repository of video lessons, e-books, PDFs, past papers, and teacher-created resources
- **Marketplace**: The section of the Content_Library where teachers publish and share paid or free content
- **Payment_Service**: The component handling M-Pesa Daraja API and bank transfer fee transactions
- **SMS_Service**: The Africa's Talking SMS gateway component for notifications
- **Analytics_Engine**: The component that processes educational data, generates insights, and flags at-risk students
- **Rust_Inference_Engine**: The high-performance Rust-based ML inference layer using the `candle` crate for edge and offline inference
- **Sync_Engine**: The Rust-based offline-first synchronization engine
- **Authentication_Service**: The W3C DID and Verifiable Credentials component managing self-sovereign identity, sessions, and role-based access
- **Blockchain_Layer**: The Polygon smart contract layer for credentials, tokens, approvals, and content registry
- **IPFS_Storage**: The decentralized content-addressed storage layer for all educational materials
- **DID**: Decentralized Identifier — W3C standard for self-sovereign identity
- **VC**: Verifiable Credential — W3C standard for tamper-proof digital credentials
- **SyncToken**: ERC-20 token for learn-to-earn economy
- **Smart_Contract**: Self-executing code on Polygon blockchain
- **Content_Repository**: The Supabase Storage-backed system for educational materials and resources
- **Audit_Log**: An immutable record of all system actions for compliance and security review
- **CBC**: Kenya's Competency-Based Curriculum framework covering PP1 through Grade 12
- **KICD**: Kenya Institute of Curriculum Development — the authority defining CBC standards
- **Lesson_Plan**: A structured teaching plan with multimedia resources (videos, PDFs, interactive quizzes)
- **Assignment**: A task assigned by teachers with submission, grading, and feedback workflows
- **Report_Card**: An automated termly or yearly academic performance report
- **Timetable**: A schedule of classes with conflict detection for time and resource allocation
- **Biometric_Integration**: System readiness for fingerprint or facial recognition attendance tracking
- **DPA_2019**: Kenya Data Protection Act 2019
- **Multilingual_Content**: Educational content available in English, Swahili, Kikuyu, Dholuo, and Luhya
- **PWA**: Progressive Web App — the offline-capable browser-based application
- **Jitsi_Meet**: The open-source video conferencing platform integrated for virtual classrooms
- **M-Pesa**: Safaricom's mobile money platform integrated via the Daraja API for fee payments
- **Africa's_Talking**: The SMS and USSD gateway provider for SMS notifications

## Requirements

### Requirement 1: Multi-Tier User Registration and Approval Workflows

**User Story:** As a new user, I want to register for the platform and be approved by the appropriate authority, so that access is controlled and the user hierarchy is maintained.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE SyncSenta_System SHALL support seven user roles in a strict hierarchy: Student, Parent, Teacher, School_Admin, School_Head, County_Officer, and National_Admin
2. WHEN a Student submits a registration request, THE Authentication_Service SHALL place the account in a pending state until a Teacher approves it
3. WHEN a Teacher submits a registration request, THE Authentication_Service SHALL place the account in a pending state until a School_Head approves it
4. WHEN a School_Admin submits a registration request, THE Authentication_Service SHALL place the account in a pending state until a School_Head approves it
5. WHEN a School_Head submits a registration request, THE Authentication_Service SHALL place the account in a pending state until a County_Officer approves it
6. WHEN a County_Officer submits a registration request, THE Authentication_Service SHALL place the account in a pending state until a National_Admin approves it
7. WHEN an approving user approves or rejects a pending registration, THE SyncSenta_System SHALL notify the applicant via email and SMS within 60 seconds
8. WHILE a user account is in pending state, THE SyncSenta_System SHALL deny access to all protected resources
9. THE Authentication_Service SHALL enforce role-based permissions ensuring each role can only access resources and actions defined for that role
10. WHEN a user provides valid credentials, THE Authentication_Service SHALL authenticate the user and create a session within 2 seconds
11. WHEN a user provides invalid credentials, THE Authentication_Service SHALL reject the login attempt and return a descriptive error message
12. THE Authentication_Service SHALL support multi-factor authentication for School_Admin, School_Head, County_Officer, and National_Admin accounts
13. WHEN a user session expires, THE SyncSenta_System SHALL require re-authentication before allowing further access

### Requirement 2: Role-Specific Dashboards and KPIs

**User Story:** As a user of any role, I want a dashboard tailored to my responsibilities, so that I can quickly access the information and actions most relevant to my role.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE SyncSenta_System SHALL provide a dedicated dashboard for each of the seven user roles with role-specific KPIs and navigation
2. WHEN a Student logs in, THE SyncSenta_System SHALL display active assignments, learning path progress, upcoming sessions, and Mwalimu_AI access
3. WHEN a Teacher logs in, THE SyncSenta_System SHALL display class performance summaries, pending grading tasks, scheme generation tools, and virtual classroom controls
4. WHEN a School_Admin logs in, THE SyncSenta_System SHALL display enrollment statistics, fee collection status, staff roster, and pending approval requests
5. WHEN a School_Head logs in, THE SyncSenta_System SHALL display school-wide performance analytics, staff approval queue, and compliance reports
6. WHEN a County_Officer logs in, THE SyncSenta_System SHALL display comparative analytics across all schools in the county and school head approval queue
7. WHEN a National_Admin logs in, THE SyncSenta_System SHALL display system-wide metrics, county-level comparisons, and full user management controls
8. WHEN a Parent logs in, THE SyncSenta_System SHALL display linked students' academic progress, attendance records, fee balances, and school event calendar

### Requirement 3: Mwalimu AI 2.0 — Adaptive Tutor

**User Story:** As a student, I want an AI tutor that understands my learning level, speaks to me in my language, and helps me through voice and text, so that I can get personalized support at any time.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a student sends a text or voice message to Mwalimu_AI, THE SyncSenta_System SHALL respond within 5 seconds
2. THE Mwalimu_AI SHALL support voice-enabled conversations using ElevenLabs eleven_v3 text-to-speech and scribe_v2 speech-to-text
3. WHEN a student speaks to Mwalimu_AI, THE SyncSenta_System SHALL transcribe the audio to text within 3 seconds before processing the query
4. THE Mwalimu_AI SHALL generate adaptive Learning_Paths based on the student's assessment performance history and identified knowledge gaps
5. WHEN a teacher saves a lesson, THE Mwalimu_AI SHALL auto-generate a quiz of at least 5 questions aligned to the lesson content within 30 seconds
6. WHEN a student uploads a PDF or document, THE Mwalimu_AI SHALL analyze the content using Gemini 3.1 Pro and answer questions about it within 10 seconds
7. WHEN a student uploads an image of a math problem or science diagram, THE Mwalimu_AI SHALL analyze the image using Gemini 3.1 Flash Image Preview and provide a step-by-step explanation
8. WHEN a student requests homework help, THE Mwalimu_AI SHALL provide step-by-step explanations without revealing the final answer directly
9. THE Mwalimu_AI SHALL support bilingual conversations switching between English and Swahili within the same session
10. WHEN a student asks a curriculum-related question, THE Mwalimu_AI SHALL provide answers aligned with official CBC content for the student's grade level using GPT-5.4
11. WHEN a student asks an off-topic question, THE Mwalimu_AI SHALL redirect the conversation to relevant educational content
12. THE Mwalimu_AI SHALL adapt response complexity based on the student's CBC grade level stored in their profile

### Requirement 4: Real-Time Virtual Classrooms

6. THE Virtual_Classroom SHALL provide chat moderation controls allowing teachers to mute participants, remove users, and manage the Q&A queue

8. WHEN a Virtual_Classroom session is scheduled, THE SyncSenta_System SHALL send calendar invites and SMS reminders to all enrolled students 24 hours and 1 hour before the session
9. WHILE a Virtual_Classroom session is active, THE SyncSenta_System SHALL display a real-time participant count and connection quality indicator to the teacher

### Requirement 6: M-Pesa and Fee Payment Management

**User Story:** As a parent or school administrator, I want to pay and track school fees using M-Pesa, so that fee transactions are convenient, transparent, and auditable.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Payment_Service SHALL integrate with the M-Pesa Daraja API to initiate and confirm STK Push payment requests
2. WHEN a parent initiates a fee payment, THE Payment_Service SHALL send an M-Pesa STK Push prompt to the parent's registered phone number within 10 seconds
3. WHEN an M-Pesa payment is confirmed by the Daraja API callback, THE SyncSenta_System SHALL update the student's fee balance and generate a payment receipt within 30 seconds
4. THE Payment_Service SHALL support bank transfer payments with manual confirmation by School_Admin
5. THE SyncSenta_System SHALL allow School_Admin to configure fee structures, due dates, and payment categories per class or grade level
6. WHEN a fee payment deadline passes with an outstanding balance, THE SMS_Service SHALL send a reminder SMS to the linked parent
7. THE SyncSenta_System SHALL provide School_Admin with exportable fee collection reports in PDF and Excel formats
8. IF an M-Pesa payment callback is not received within 60 seconds, THEN THE Payment_Service SHALL query the Daraja API transaction status and reconcile the result
